Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 5, 2025

Your Privacy Matters

TVStats is designed with your privacy as a top priority. The app works completely offline and does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information or usage data.

No Data Collection

TVStats does not collect any data from your device. All system monitoring and statistics are processed locally on your Apple TV and remain on your device at all times.

Fully Offline Operation

The app operates entirely offline and does not require an internet connection to function. No data is ever sent to external servers or third parties.

What This Means for You

  • No personal information is collected or stored
  • No usage data, analytics, or telemetry is gathered
  • No internet connection is required for the app to work
  • No data is shared with third parties
  • No advertising or tracking technologies are used
  • All system information displayed remains local to your device

System Information Access

TVStats accesses system information such as CPU usage, memory statistics, and performance metrics solely to display them to you within the app. This information never leaves your device.

Changes to This Policy

If we make any changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy or TVStats, please feel free to reach out.
